Why we exist

Most products sold as healthy, clean, or safe contain ingredients that are none of those things.

Everything on this site has already been screened. If it's here, it passed — so you never have to check.

What we screen out — food

Seed oils · High fructose corn syrup · Artificial sweeteners · Carrageenan · Hidden "natural flavours" · Artificial colours · BHT/BHA · Sodium benzoate · and more.

What we screen out — skincare

Parabens · Synthetic fragrance · SLS/SLES · Phthalates · PEGs · Formaldehyde releasers · Oxybenzone · Triclosan · Mineral oil · and more.

What we screen out — supplements

Proprietary blends · Titanium dioxide · Artificial colours · Carrageenan · Undisclosed fillers · Magnesium stearate · Silicon dioxide · Hydrogenated oils · and more.
